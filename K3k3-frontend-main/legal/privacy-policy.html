Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

Introduction

At K3K3 Ride, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our ride-hailing platform.

Our Commitment: We believe in transparency and giving you control over your personal information. This policy is designed to help you understand our practices and make informed decisions.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that identifies you as an individual:

  • Name and Contact Details: Full name, phone number, email address
  • Identification Documents: Driver's license, national ID for verification
  • Payment Information: Mobile money accounts and payment preferences
  • Profile Photos: For identification and safety purposes

Location Data

  • Current Location: GPS data for ride matching and pickup
  • Route Information: Pickup and destination addresses
  • Real-time Tracking: Location during active rides for safety

Usage Data

  • App Usage: How you interact with our platform
  • Ride History: Past trips, routes, and preferences
  • Device Information: Phone model, operating system, app version
  • Performance Data: App crashes, loading times, errors

Communications

  • Customer Support: Messages, calls, and support requests
  • Notifications: Ride updates, promotions, and alerts
  • Feedback: Ratings, reviews, and survey responses

Information Sharing

1. When We Share Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Drivers: Name, pickup location, and contact for ride coordination
  • Service Providers: Payment processors, mapping services, analytics providers
  • Legal Requirements: Law enforcement, regulatory authorities, court orders
  • Business Transfers: Mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Payment Processing: Secure payment gateways
  • Mapping & Navigation: GPS and routing services
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Cloud Storage: Secure data hosting
Important: We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. All data sharing is strictly for service provision and legal compliance.

Data Security

1. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Employee Training: Privacy and security awareness programs

2. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Account Information: Until account deletion
  • Ride History: 2 years for safety and dispute resolution
  • Payment Records: 7 years for financial compliance
  • Support Communications: 1 year for service improvement

Your Rights

1. Data Subject Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your personal information
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another service
  • Objection: Limit processing of your information
  • Restriction: Suspend certain processing activities

2. Exercising Your Rights

To exercise your rights:

  • Use the privacy settings in your account
  • Contact our privacy team at privacy@k3k3ride.com
  • Submit a formal request through our help center
  • We respond to all requests within 30 days

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Ghana. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses with service providers
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws
  • Security assessments of international transfers

Children's Privacy

K3K3 Ride is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will:

  • Post changes on our website and app
  • Notify users of significant changes
  • Update the "Last Updated" date
  • Provide a summary of key changes
Your Responsibility: Please review this policy periodically. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
