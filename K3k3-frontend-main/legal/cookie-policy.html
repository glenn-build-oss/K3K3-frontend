Cookie Policy

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how K3K3 Ride uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

What are Cookies? Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Category
Authentication Cookies Keep you logged in and secure your account Session / 30 days Essential
Preference Cookies Remember your language and settings 1 year Functional
Analytics Cookies Help us improve our services 1 year Analytics
Marketing Cookies Show relevant promotions 30 days Marketing
Location Cookies Enable GPS and ride matching Session Essential

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our platform to function:

  • User authentication and session management
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Load balancing and service stability
  • Location services for ride matching

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Save your payment methods securely
  • Customize your ride experience
  • Language and regional settings

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us improve:

  • Analytics and usage statistics
  • Performance monitoring
  • Error tracking and debugging
  • A/B testing results

Your Cookie Choices

1. Managing Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Block or delete cookies through your browser
  • Cookie Consent: Accept or reject non-essential cookies
  • App Settings: Manage cookies within our mobile app
  • Third-party Tools: Use privacy tools to control tracking

2. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling essential cookies may:

  • Prevent you from logging in to your account
  • Affect ride booking and GPS functionality
  • Limit access to certain features
  • Reduce personalization options

Cookie Duration

Session Cookies

These cookies expire when you close your browser or app and are used for:

  • Temporary session management
  • Security tokens
  • Current ride information

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period:

  • User preferences: 1 year
  • Analytics data: 1 year
  • Marketing cookies: 30 days
  • Authentication tokens: 30 days

Third-Party Cookies

We work with trusted third-party services that may use cookies:

Service Providers

  • Payment Processors: Secure payment processing
  • Mapping Services: GPS and navigation
  • Analytics Providers: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Customer Support: Chat and help systems

Third-Party Control

You can control third-party cookies through:

  • Your browser's privacy settings
  • Third-party privacy preferences
  • Opt-out tools and services

Cookie Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with a new date
  • Communicated through our app notifications
  • Highlighted in our newsletter for significant changes
  • Effective immediately upon posting
Your Responsibility: Please review this policy periodically. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are cookies safe?

Yes, cookies are generally safe. They cannot harm your device or access personal information without your permission.

Do you use tracking cookies?

We only use tracking for analytics and service improvement. We don't sell your data to third parties.

Can I use K3K3 without cookies?

Essential cookies are required for basic functionality, but you can opt out of non-essential cookies.

How do I clear cookies?

You can clear cookies through your browser settings or app preferences.
